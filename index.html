Your Privacy

Your Catches. Your Photos. Your Data.

Everything you log in Fish Tales™ — your catches, your photos, your fish holes, your patterns — exists for one reason only: to power your experience in the app.

We don't analyze your data to sell insights. We don't share your information with advertisers. We don't use your photos for any purpose beyond displaying them to you. There is no third-party tracking, no ad network, no data broker relationship of any kind.

Fish Tales™ is funded entirely by Pro subscriptions — not advertisers. Your diary is yours.
